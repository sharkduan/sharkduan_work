# CovalentInDB 2.0 Field Priority

The first CovalentInDB 2.0 integration will parse inhibitor identity, target identity, UniProt or target names, PDB or cocrystal structure identifiers, covalent binding site, warhead type, and covalent reaction mechanism. Activity, assay, clinical status, and noncovalent interaction annotations may be preserved but will not enter the first training loss. ADMET predictions, virtual screening library compounds, and covalent natural product collections are deferred to later filtering or ranking workflows.
